Corn Tortillas

Homemade tortilla shells to be stuffed with goodness

Unfortunately, I have no idea how to cook. However, I am required to publish a small recipe website. For example, one of the ingredients is masa harina. I don't have a clue what that is.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups masa harina
  • 1 ⅛ cups hot water

Steps

  1. Mix it in a bowl
  2. Knead it
  3. Cover with plastic wrap
  4. Sit at room temp for 30 minutes
  5. Divide dough into 15 balls
  6. Press each ball flat between two sheets of plastic wrap
  7. Cook in pan for about 30 seconds per side
  8. Fill 'em, roll 'em up and eat 'em yum!
