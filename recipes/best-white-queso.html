Best White Queso

This should be similar to the queso you get at authentic Mexican resaurants

Unfortunately, I have no idea how to cook. However, I am required to publish a small recipe website. For the love of god, do not attempt to follow any of my recipes if you value your intestinal fortitude.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb white American cheese
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tspn cumin
  • 2 tspn garlic powder
  • 2 tspn onion powder
  • cayenne pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Throw cheese, milk and butter in a saucepan and melt 'em
  2. Add in all the powders and cayenne
  3. Stir it up à la Bob Marley
  4. Cook until heated through
  5. Eat 'em up, yum!
