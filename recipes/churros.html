Churros

These are crispy, cinnamonny Mexican fritters

Unfortunately, I have no idea how to cook. However, I am required to publish a small recipe website. Follow this and any of my other recipes at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • water
  • sugar
  • salt
  • oil
  • flour
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Boil sugar, water, oil & salt
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the flour
  3. Stuff the dough in a pastry bag and extrude some strips
  4. Fry 'em up in hot oil 'til golden
  5. Drain the oil and roll 'em in the cinnamon
  6. Eat 'em up yum!
